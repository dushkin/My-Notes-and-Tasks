Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Notes & Tasks ("we", "us", "our") operates https://notask.co and our mobile web app (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you register, we collect your email address.

Usage Data: We automatically collect data about your interactions (e.g., pages visited, features used).

Payment Data: For paid plans, payment is processed via Paddle; we receive limited billing metadata (plan type, transaction ID) but not your full payment details.

Cookies & Tracking: We use cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences and track usage. You can control cookie settings in your browser.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We may share information with:

We retain your account data for as long as needed to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

6. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no internet transmission is completely secure.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, contact us to remove it.

8. International Transfers

As a global service, data may be transferred to and stored on servers outside your jurisdiction. By using the Service, you consent to such transfers.

9. Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your account information

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@notask.co.

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will post the revised version with a new "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
